About

Turning briefs
into icons.

I'm Kamesh — a photographer and art director with over a decade of experience creating campaign imagery for advertising agencies and direct brands across fashion, beauty, automotive, and hospitality.

My work sits at the intersection of commercial precision and visual poetry. I believe every brand has an untold visual story — I find it, frame it, and make it impossible to forget.

I collaborate with creative directors, agencies, and in-house teams from concept through final delivery, bringing both a photographer's eye and an art director's strategic thinking to every project.
